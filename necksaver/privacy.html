Postrly — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-16

Postrly ("the app") is a macOS utility that uses your Mac's camera to remind you about your sitting posture. Camera frames never leave your Mac.

1. Data we do not collect

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not create an account, ask for an email, or send any data to any server. There are no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or attribution SDKs.

While monitoring is active, Postrly accesses your camera and analyzes your head posture in real time on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Camera frames are processed in memory and discarded immediately — never saved to disk, never screenshotted, never recorded, and never transmitted.

2. What is stored locally on your Mac

  • First-launch timestamp (used to compute trial days remaining).
  • Subscription receipt (issued and verified by Apple, not by us).
  • Your preferences — reminder frequency, launch-at-login, onboarding-completed flag.

This information stays on your Mac and is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Notifications

Postrly posts macOS notifications only when poor posture or prolonged sitting is detected. Notifications contain no advertising and no third-party content.

4. Subscriptions and payment

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Postrly never receives or stores your payment information, billing address, or Apple ID. See Apple's privacy policy for the data Apple handles.

5. Permissions we request

  • Camera: required for posture monitoring. Only used when monitoring is active. You can revoke this at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.
  • Notifications: required for reminder banners. Revocable at any time in System Settings → Notifications.

6. Third parties

Postrly does not integrate any third-party SDK. The only network interaction is standard StoreKit communication between your Mac and Apple to validate your subscription; this is handled by Apple, not by us.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not collect any personal data from any user.

8. Your rights

You can at any time revoke camera or notification permissions, cancel your subscription through the App Store, or uninstall the app to remove its local storage. Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no data for us to export, correct, or delete on our side.

9. Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date and post the new version at this URL. The copy embedded inside the app will also be updated in the next release.
