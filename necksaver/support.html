Common questions

The app says my camera is blocked

Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera and enable Postrly. Then restart the app.

Reminders stopped appearing

Open System Settings → Notifications → Postrly and make sure notifications are allowed. Also check that you haven't set a long cooldown in the app's Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store app → click your account icon → Subscriptions → Postrly → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support.

Does the app upload my camera feed?

No. All posture analysis runs locally on your Mac using Apple's Vision framework. Frames are never saved or transmitted. See our Privacy Policy for the full statement.
